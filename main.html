Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning vocabulary?
Simply select a lesson from the "Let's Learn Vocabularies" section. Each lesson contains words with meanings and pronunciations to help you learn easily.
What happens when I click a lesson button?
When you click a lesson button, all vocabulary words related to that lesson will appear in the vocabulary section with their meanings and pronunciations.
How can I see detailed information about a word?
Click the information icon on any word card. A popup will open showing the meaning, pronunciation, example sentence, and synonyms.
How does the search feature work?
You can type any word in the search box and click the search button. The system will display all matching vocabulary words instantly.
Can I learn pronunciation of words here?
Yes. Each word includes its pronunciation to help you understand how the word is spoken in English.
Is this platform free to use?
Yes. English Janala is completely free and designed to help learners improve their English vocabulary easily.
